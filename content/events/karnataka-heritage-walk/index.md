Join our Karnataka chapter for an early-morning heritage walk exploring the architectural legacy of Shivajinagar. A knowledgeable local guide will lead participants through significant buildings, public squares, and hidden courtyards.

## Details

- **Meeting point:** Outside Shivajinagar Bus Stand, Platform 1
- **Duration:** Approximately 2.5 hours
- **What to bring:** Comfortable footwear, water bottle, curiosity
- **Group size:** Limited to 20 participants

Registration is required to secure your spot.
