Mark World Environment Day with the Karnataka chapter. We gather at Ulsoor Lake for a morning of tree planting, a short lake clean-up drive, and short talks from local environmentalists.

## Programme

- 8:00 AM — Gathering and registration
- 8:30 AM — Lake walkway clean-up drive
- 10:00 AM — Tree plantation at the lake bund (saplings provided)
- 11:00 AM — Talks: urban biodiversity and citizen action
- 12:30 PM — Refreshments and networking

Wear clothes you don't mind getting muddy. Gloves and bags will be provided for the clean-up.
