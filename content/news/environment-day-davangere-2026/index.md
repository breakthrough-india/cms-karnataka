Breakthrough Science Society, Davanagere in association with Puneeth Rajkumar PU College, Davanagere, and Sub-Regional Science Centre Davanagere jointly organized Environment Day on the 5th of June 2026 at the auditorium of the Science Centre.

Environment Day program commenced with the planting of saplings, followed by a series of guest lectures.

[![](https://pub-d125678e734647aeb774df3e25dd6abc.r2.dev/2026/06/environment-day-davangere-2026-img2.jpg)](https://pub-d125678e734647aeb774df3e25dd6abc.r2.dev/2026/06/environment-day-davangere-2026-img2.jpg)

The program was inaugurated by Shri B.N. Mallesh, Administrative Officer of Puneeth Rajkumar PU College. In his address, he stated that celebrating Environment Day shouldn’t just be about planting a sapling on that specific day; instead, we must incorporate several changes into our daily lives to ensure our actions complement the environment. He emphasized that to achieve this, we first need to develop and strengthen a scientific temperament within ourselves, and noted that the lecture sessions organized today would be helpful in this regard.

Speaking as the chief guest of the event, Shri Anand Raj, President of the Breakthrough Science Society, expressed that along with individual efforts to correct climate anomalies, strict policy regulations must be implemented at both national and international levels.

Another guest of the program, Dr. Nataraj, Associate Professor at the Government First Grade College, spoke about focusing on sustainable change. He remarked that it is the duty of all of us to protect, nurture, and safely pass on our environment to the next generation, adding that the program becomes truly meaningful if we take a pledge toward this goal today.

Serving as the resource persons for the event, Dr. S. Shishupala, Professor in the Department of Microbiology at Davanagere University, Dr. Sampath Kumar K., Senior Biology Lecturer at a Government Pre-University College, and Dr. Vasudhendra, a Breakthrough Advisor and expert, delivered lectures to the children regarding the adverse effects of climate change on living organisms.

Dr. Santhosh, Principal of Puneeth Rajkumar PU College, delivered the introductory remarks for the program, and Mahalakshmi hosted the event.
