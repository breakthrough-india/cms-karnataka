The camp aimed to instill a scientific temper, encourage a questioning attitude, and create awareness against superstitions among children.

Around 40 children enthusiastically participated in the event.

**Key Highlights of the Sessions:**

**Scientific Temper:** Shri Anand Raj (Karnataka State President, Breakthrough Science Society) spoke on the importance of developing a scientific temper and logical thinking.

**Fun Experiments:** Shri Beeranna Salagara (District Organizer) demonstrated various scientific principles through entertaining experiments related to water, light, and chemistry.

**Combating Superstitions:** Prof. Azeem Jamadar from Ilkal spoke on the necessity of scientific thinking, emphasizing that nothing should be followed blindly without understanding the underlying reasons.

**Inspirational Values:** Ms. Bangari Patil (Teacher) spoke in detail about the life principles and values of Albert Einstein. Shri Vittal Dalavayi (Teacher & CRP) delivered an inspiring talk on the life ideals, achievements, and human values of great personalities.

**Concluding Session:** During the valedictory ceremony, Shri Gururaj Doddamani (Teacher & District Organizer) educated the children on the significance of science and the mission of the Breakthrough Science Society.

The camp also included an engaging session of interactive games and activities conducted by Ms Saroja (Teacher)
